Francis Bacon

Painter

Here's a time line of Francis Bacon's life:

  • 1909 - Born in Dublin, Ireland
  • 1930s - Picasso-influenced bio-morphs and Furies.
  • 1940s - male heads isolated in rooms or geometric structures
  • 1950s - "screaming popes,
  • mid-to-late 1950s animals and lone figures
  • 1960s - crucifixions
  • mid-to-late 1960s - portraits of friends
  • 1970s - self-portraits,
  • 1980s - and the cooler, more technical paintings.
  • 1992 - dies at the age of 82. Madrid, Spain.
